Privacy Policy

Welcome to CashVibe. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application or website ("Platform").

By using CashVibe, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

1. Information We Collect

To provide and improve our service, we collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Data: Name, Email address, and Mobile Number collected during registration.
  • Financial Data: UPI ID or Wallet details provided strictly for processing your reward withdrawals.
  • Device Information: We collect your Device ID, IP Address, OS type, and Browser version. This is strictly used for Fraud Prevention (to prevent multiple accounts on a single device).
  • App Activity: Task completion status, ad interactions, and spin wheel usage to calculate your rewards.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly for operational and security purposes:

  • To create and manage your CashVibe account.
  • To track task completions and credit virtual coins to your wallet.
  • To process real-money payouts to your requested UPI/Wallet.
  • To detect and block fraudulent activities, bots, or multiple account creation.
  • To send you push notifications regarding your rewards or app updates (via OneSignal).

3. Third-Party Services & Trackers

CashVibe is a micro-earning platform. We partner with third-party advertisers and offerwalls. These third parties may collect data to verify task completions:

  • Offerwalls & Ad Networks: Services like CPAGrip, CPALead, Adsterra, etc. They may track your IP address and device interactions to verify app installs or survey completions.
  • Firebase (Google): Used for database management, authentication, and secure hosting.
  • OneSignal: Used for sending push notifications.

These third-party providers have their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information.

4. Data Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. Your data is securely stored on Google Firebase servers. Passwords are cryptographically hashed. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable.

5. Account Deletion & Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can contact our support team. Upon deletion, your personal information, balance, and device footprint will be permanently erased.

6. Children's Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.
