Refund & Cancellation Policy

Refund Policy

Thank you for using CashVibe. This policy outlines our guidelines for refunds, cancellations, and disputes regarding virtual coins and real-money payouts.

1. General Earning & Payout Rule

CashVibe is a micro-earning platform where users earn virtual "Coins" by completing tasks, watching ads, or playing games. These coins can be redeemed for real cash (UPI/Wallets). Since users do not pay CashVibe to use the platform, traditional purchase refunds do not apply.

2. Game Arena (Entry Fees)

If you participate in our "Game Arena" (e.g., Tic Tac Toe, Quiz):

  • Virtual coins are deducted as an entry fee before the match starts.
  • If a match results in a DRAW, 100% of your entry coins will be automatically refunded to your wallet.
  • If the opponent fails to join (Matchmaking Timeout), 100% of your entry coins are instantly refunded.
  • If you deliberately quit, disconnect, or close the app during an active match, you will forfeit your entry fee. No refunds will be provided for user-side disconnections.

3. Withdrawal Cancellations & Rejections

When you request a real-money payout, our system reviews your account activity:

  • If your withdrawal is REJECTED due to incorrect UPI details, the equivalent coins will be refunded back to your CashVibe wallet.
  • If your withdrawal is rejected due to fraudulent activity (e.g., VPN usage, fake referrals, auto-clickers), your coins will be permanently confiscated, your account will be banned, and no refund will be issued.

4. App Errors & Glitches

If you lose coins due to a proven technical glitch or server error on our end, please contact support within 48 hours. Upon verification, we will restore the lost coins to your account.

5. Contact Us
